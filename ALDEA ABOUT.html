About Us

ALDEA Events & Catering

Welcome to ALDEA Events & Catering - formerly known as G.A. Miranda Party Needs, your trusted partner in creating memorable celebrations since 2010. We specialize in providing high-quality party supplies and decorations for all types of events, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations.

From G.A. Miranda to ALDEA - Still the same magic! With years of experience in the party needs industry, we understand that every event is unique and deserves special attention to detail. Our team is dedicated to helping you bring your vision to life.
